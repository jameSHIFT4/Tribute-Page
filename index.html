A brief overview of Mang0

Joseph "Mang0" Marquez is an American Melee Falco and Fox main. With over 30 major tournament wins, he is one of the Five Gods and is considered one of the greatest Melee players of all time. Ranked 8th in SSBMRank 2023, he is considered both the best Falco player and one of the best Fox players in the world.
